About MW Lost & Found

MW Lost & Found is a trusted Malawi-based platform that bridges the gap between people who lose items and those who find them.

Our platform provides:

  • Fast and easy reporting of lost and found items
  • Secure communication via WhatsApp to protect privacy
  • Real-time notifications and alerts for higher recovery chances
  • Mobile-friendly interface, accessible on all devices
  • Location-based matching to connect nearby users quickly
  • Verified user interactions to ensure safety and trust

Our mission is to make item recovery in Malawi simple, efficient, and secure for everyone.
